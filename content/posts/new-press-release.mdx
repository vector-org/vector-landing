**Zürich**, **Switzerland** – 11th November 2025: VECTOR, a next-generation game distribution platform that fixes what's wrong with Web2 gaming using the best of Web3 technology, has announced the launch of its **Closed Alpha**. Guided by its mission, “**Your Keys - Your Games,”** VECTOR is building a future where players truly own what they buy, and developers earn fairly from every sale and resale.

After extensive internal testing, the VECTOR client is now opening its doors to a select group of early participants, including **industry partners, pre-existing funders, and recipients of the limited “VECTOR Metal Card” distributed at recent events.**

The Closed Alpha will feature two distinct game experiences:

* *Lyra on Vector*: Unreal Engine’s sandbox shooter, reimagined to include the VECTOR Cat Luis and VECTOR Dog Mortimer, the company’s mascots, brought to life in-game. This is a test for the client, showcasing VECTOR’s integration of assets and identity within existing game frameworks.
* *The Last Drop (Demo):* developed by Lohkare Games Oy, a Finnish studio known for crafting ambitious open-world experiences. The Last Drop is an upcoming action title slated for release in early 2026. The partnership allows VECTOR to showcase how its client supports seamless integration with third-party studios, enabling players to experience genuine ownership from day one.

These two vastly different titles will allow testers to explore VECTOR’s purchase, installation, and gameplay systems firsthand, demonstrating how the platform bridges traditional gaming familiarity with blockchain-backed on-chain ownership.

Following its successful showcase at **Gamescom 2025**, the VECTOR team continues to expand its presence at major global industry events. This month, the company will participate at **DevConnect in Buenos Aires** and **Unite in Barcelona**, engaging directly with developers and studios as the platform advances toward open testing phases.

> “This milestone is truly the beginning of a greater journey,” said Oliver Scholl, CEO of VECTOR. “The Closed Alpha is the first step in showing how game ownership can evolve, giving players real value and developers a sustainable way to benefit from secondary markets.”

With this Closed Alpha, VECTOR is inviting early collaborators to shape the future of player-owned gaming ecosystems, built on transparency, interoperability, and sovereignty.

**About VECTOR**: VECTOR is a next-generation game distribution platform that fixes what's wrong with Web2 gaming using the best of Web3 technology. The platform enables true digital ownership and resale of games and in-game assets, giving players control over their libraries while providing developers with a new, sustainable revenue model.
